Smart Bra for Breast Cancer Monitoring

SmartBra Device for Breast Cancer Detection and Monitoring. Self-diagnosis,Affordable and User Friendly health solution for women.

Product
Investor

Background

A Smart clothing, Convenient, User friendly and Cost Effective technology which uses non- invasive, painless method based on frequent ultrasound monitoring that is designed to detect earliest stage cancer and monitor the daily condition of a diagnosed breast cancer patient.

Concept

A Smart Clothing embedded with Piezoelctric transducers that sends ultrasounds from multiple sources to generate image of inside breast using sound reflections. The device accurately and safely detects and monitors potential cancerous cells at an early stage from the comfort of home.

Smart Wears

Early cancer detection and monitoring from home

Self screening

Aids self examination and avoids frequent hospital visits for routine examinations

Ultrasound Technology

No compression, radiation, or contrast materials

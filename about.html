About our Company

We are the first wearable/fashion technology company in Nigeria, designing and developing technology worn close to the body by embedding programmable electronics and sensors on clothing to solve issues-related to health.

Vision

With our wearable devices, we want to help improve the daily lives of at least one million people before 2030.

Engr. Bolarinwa Kemisola

Founder & CEO

An Inventor, Innovator, and entrepreneur, She's a Robotics and Embedded System Engineer with an exceptional tech skills. Her expertise spans across Robotics, Automation, Embedded C, Wearable Tech and IoT with 9 years experience.
